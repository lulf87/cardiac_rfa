# Cover letter (v5)

Dear Editor,

Please find enclosed our revised manuscript entitled **"Uncertainty-aware reduced 2D electro-thermal modeling of transmural lesion formation during radiofrequency cardiac ablation"** for consideration in *Computer Methods and Programs in Biomedicine*.

This manuscript presents a reduced two-dimensional electro-thermal model for comparative analysis of standard RF, HPSD, and vHPSD protocols under uncertain contact and cooling conditions. The central contribution is an uncertainty-aware framework that combines deterministic lesion-geometry summaries with transmurality and overheating probability maps, together with explicit confidence-interval reporting.

In the present revision we have: (i) corrected the assembled figure assets and figure/caption consistency; (ii) typeset the governing expressions and surrogate rules as equations in the manuscript; (iii) expanded the output metrics to include lesion width, lesion area, and depth-to-width ratio; (iv) clarified the uncertainty design and Wilson-score confidence intervals; and (v) expanded the reference list substantially.

We believe that the manuscript fits the aims of the journal as a methodology-focused computational study in biomedical engineering. The manuscript has not been published elsewhere and is not under consideration by another journal.
